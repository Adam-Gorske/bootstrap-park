ORIGINS OF THE LANDSCAPE

The painted hills were born from fire and ash. Millions of years before you and I walked this planet, Oregon was home to a healthy amount of volcanoes. Year after year lava, ash, and sulfuric gasses leached from the earth's crust, and poured out into the landscape that is today known as the Painted Hills. Geologists have discovered creatures such as the wooly mammoth, saber tooth tiger, lach ness monster, and sasquatch near the Painted Hills. Every year a few instagram influencers head to Eastern Oregon to try and get famous. Some do, but most don't. As more people come into the area, the landscape changes. Please remember this before you embark on your journey.

GETTING HERE

There's not a lot out here besides the Painted Hills, but if you do decide to come out this way, car travel is probably your best bet. The closest train station is West World, but compared to the coast, we're east.
